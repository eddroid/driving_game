Retro-Educational Driving Game

Welcome to DriverGamer.com!


To play, simply select the correct GO or YIELD button for each driving situation.

Driving rules:

  • Yield to person on right, except if turning right.
  • Yield to oncoming traffic when turning left.

Remember... the car on the RIGHT has RIGHT-OF-WAY.
